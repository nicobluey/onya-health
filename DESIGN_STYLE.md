# Onya Health Design Style

**Overview**
Warm, editorial healthcare aesthetic with sand-toned foundations, bark-dark typography, sunlight highlights, and forest accents. Interfaces are calm, confident, and tactile with generous radius and layered soft shadows.

**Typography**
- Body: `Inter` for UI and long-form readability.
- Display: `Playfair Display` as the Exposure-style serif for headlines and hero statements.
- Heading tone: high-contrast, tight tracking, short lines.

**Color Tokens**
- `--color-bark-900` `#28030f` primary text and primary button base.
- `--color-bark-700` `#5b3e47` secondary text.
- `--color-bark-500` `#8a7078` tertiary text and subtle UI hints.
- `--color-sand-25` `#fcfaf8` page background.
- `--color-sand-50` `#f9f4f1` section background.
- `--color-sand-200` `#f0dfd1` borders and dividers.
- `--color-sunlight-100` `#fdfac4` hero glow and warmth accents.
- `--color-sunlight-200` `#fbf582` highlight rings and chips.
- `--color-forest-700` `#2b6433` success accents and status.

**Surfaces**
- Base: `bg-sand-25` for the full page.
- Section blocks: `bg-sand-50` or `bg-white` when you want cards to pop.
- Highlights: `bg-sunlight-50` and `bg-sunlight-100` for featured areas.

**Components**
- Primary button: bark background, sand text, rounded-xl or rounded-3xl for hero CTAs.
- Outline button: bark border, transparent fill, sand hover.
- Cards: white background, sand border, rounded-2xl/3xl, soft shadow.
- Status/verification chips: sunlight background with bark text.
- Icons: forest for “success”/trust, sunlight for rating stars.

**Layout**
- Generous vertical rhythm: `py-16` to `py-24` for primary sections.
- Max content width: 1200–1280px with `px-6` to `px-8` gutters.
- Emphasize two-column hero layouts and centered testimonial grids.

**Motion**
- Subtle fades and short vertical transitions.
- Favor ease-out, 200–400ms, no bounce except for micro highlights.

**Imagery**
- Friendly, clinical portraits with soft edges and large radii.
- Use warm sunlight backplates and blurred ambient shapes for depth.
