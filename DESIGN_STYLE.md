# Onya Health Design Style

## Overview
Clean, trustworthy telehealth aesthetic using white surfaces, sky-blue actions, and high-contrast charcoal typography. Components are soft-cornered and structured to feel modern without visual clutter.

## Typography
- Display and headings: `"Basic Commercial Pro", Arial, sans-serif`
- Body and UI text: `Inter, system-ui, sans-serif`
- Heading tone: bold, concise, high contrast.
- Body tone: calm, legible, and practical.

## Color Guidelines
- Primary blue: `#2e8cff`
- Primary hover: `#1f7be6`
- Text primary: `#020617`
- Text secondary: `#334155`
- Base white surface: `#ffffff`
- Border: `#cbd5e1`
- Light neutral fill: `#f8fafc` / `#f1f5f9`
- Pending/warning: `#d97706`

## Component Direction
- Buttons: rounded, high-contrast, minimal chrome.
- Cards: white/sand fills with subtle border and restrained shadows.
- Sections: generous spacing with clear visual grouping.
- Footer: rounded organic container, dark contrast block, compact link columns.

## Motion
- Smooth and restrained transitions.
- Sticky story sections are acceptable when they improve narrative flow.
- Keep hover/scroll effects supportive, not decorative.

## Imagery
- Hero photography should prioritize people over background scenery.
- Maintain readable contrast for text overlays.
- Avoid heavy color filters that distort skin tones or brand colors.
