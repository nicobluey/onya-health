# Affordable telehealth without compromising quality

Patients should not have to choose between affordability and quality. Telehealth can deliver both when the model is designed well.

## What quality telehealth looks like

Quality care online means more than a video call.

- Clear intake and symptom capture
- Appropriate clinical review by registered clinicians
- Fast, secure documentation and follow-up

## Why affordability matters

Cost is one of the biggest barriers to care. Predictable pricing and efficient online pathways reduce that barrier for more Australians.

When care is affordable, patients are more likely to seek help early instead of waiting until issues worsen.

## Better access across Australia

Telehealth supports people in major cities and regional areas alike.

With online intake and digital delivery, patients can get reviewed and supported without travel, queues, or avoidable delays.

## The Onya approach

We focus on practical outcomes: fast access, transparent process, and clinician-reviewed care that patients can trust.
