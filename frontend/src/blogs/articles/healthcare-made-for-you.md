# Healthcare made for you: how AI improves patient matching

Choosing care should feel simple, not stressful. At Onya, we use AI to match each patient with the right clinician based on symptoms, goals, and context.

## Why matching matters

The best outcome is not just fast care. It is care with the right clinician from the start.

- Symptom-led routing helps direct people to the right specialty.
- Better matching reduces repeated intake and handoff delays.
- Patients get a clearer and more personalised care journey.

## What this means for patients

When matching is done well, patients spend less time deciding where to go and more time getting help.

For example, someone needing a work medical certificate can be routed differently from someone needing nutrition support or mental health care.

## Human clinicians stay in control

AI supports triage and routing. Clinical decisions remain with qualified Australian clinicians.

That means technology improves speed and experience while care quality stays grounded in professional standards.

## The goal

Healthcare should adapt to the patient, not the other way around.

Our goal is simple: healthcare made for you.
