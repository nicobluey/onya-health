# How to choose the right online clinician

Not every health concern needs the same type of support. Choosing the right clinician is the first step to better outcomes.

## Start with your goal

Ask: what am I trying to resolve today?

- Medical certificate or short-term symptom review
- Ongoing nutrition and lifestyle support
- Mental health care and structured psychological support

## Look for the right fit

A good match combines clinical capability with communication style and care preferences.

The right clinician should make next steps clear, practical, and easy to follow.

## Why AI-assisted matching helps

AI can improve the first routing decision by using symptom and intent signals from intake responses.

This reduces friction and helps patients avoid being bounced between services.

## Final reminder

Online care works best when symptoms are suitable for telehealth and when patients receive clear follow-up instructions.

If symptoms are severe or urgent, emergency care should be accessed immediately.
